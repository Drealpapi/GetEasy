TO ADD YOUR SPLASH VIDEO:

1. Place your video file here and name it "splash-video.mp4"
2. Open App.tsx and change the import:
   FROM: import SplashScreenSimple from "./src/screens/SplashScreenSimple";
   TO:   import SplashScreen from "./src/screens/SplashScreen";

3. Update the component in App.tsx:
   FROM: <SplashScreenSimple onFinish={handleSplashFinish} />
   TO:   <SplashScreen onFinish={handleSplashFinish} />

4. Open src/screens/SplashScreen.tsx and uncomment the Video component

Your video should be:
- MP4 format
- 7 seconds or less
- Portrait orientation preferred
- Under 10MB file size

The app will automatically play your video for 7 seconds then show the role selection screen.
